SPACE AWARENESS

Real-time orbital tracking of satellites, space stations, and orbital debris using client-side SGP4 propagation.

DATA SOURCES

The GEOINT.WORLD Space Data module performs orbital propagation using high-precision TLE datasets.

CELESTRAK (NORAD)

Primary source for Two-Line Element sets (TLEs) describing the orbit of Earth-orbiting objects.

Positions are calculated locally in your browser using the SGP4 (Simplified General Perturbations) propagator. Accuracy is typically within a few kilometers but depends on the "freshness" of the TLE epoch.

ORBITAL CLASSES

The system tracks thousands of objects categorized into specific functional groups:

SATELITES

Active payloads including Weather (NOAA/GOES), Navigation (GPS/GLONASS/GALILEO), and Communications (Starlink/OneWeb/Iridium).

STATIONS

Large manned and unmanned platforms, including the International Space Station (ISS) and the Tiangong Space Station (CSS).

DEBRIS & ROCKETS

Spent rocket bodies and tracked debris. We specifically monitor "Last 30 Days" launches for new objects entering orbit.

IDENTIFICATION

VISUALIZATION

Objects are plotted at their sub-satellite point (the point on Earth directly below the satellite).

  • Altitude: Height above the WGS84 ellipsoid (in km/mi).
  • Velocity: Orbital speed relative to Earth's center.
