WATCH LIVE

Stream live news broadcasts from major networks directly within the GEOINT interface.

OVERVIEW

The Watch Live feature aggregates real-time video streams from news channels worldwide, allowing you to monitor breaking news while tracking global events on the map.

Data Source: Live streams are provided by the tk.gg Live News API, which aggregates publicly available YouTube live news broadcasts.

HOW TO USE

To access live news streams:

  1. Toggle WATCH LIVE in the NEWS section of the control panel
  2. A modal will appear showing all available live streams
  3. Check the box next to any channel to pop it out as a floating player
  4. Multiple streams can be active simultaneously

FLOATING PLAYER CONTROLS

Each active stream appears as a draggable, resizable video player overlay:

  • DRAG — Click and hold the header bar to move the player
  • RESIZE — Drag any corner handle to resize the player
  • CLOSE — Click the [X] button to close the stream
  • INTERACT — Click anywhere on the video to enable YouTube controls

CHANNEL LIST

The modal displays available streams with the following information:

  • CHANNEL NAME — The news network broadcasting
  • STREAM TITLE — Current broadcast title or topic
  • VIEWERS — Live concurrent viewer count (e.g., "12.5K")

Streams are sorted by viewer count by default, showing the most popular broadcasts first.

FEATURES

MULTI-STREAM

Watch multiple news channels simultaneously in separate floating windows.

AUTO REFRESH

Channel list refreshes every 5 minutes to show current streams and viewer counts.

GLOBAL COVERAGE

Access news broadcasts from networks around the world in various languages.

TIPS

  • Stack multiple players in corners to monitor several broadcasts at once
  • Resize players to fit your workflow — make one large and others smaller
  • Use with the NEWS FEED and PREDICTION FEED for comprehensive situational awareness
  • Click the REFRESH button to manually update the channel list if needed
