2. Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

2.1 Data Collection
GEOINT.WORLD is a static client-side application. We do not store personal user data, accounts, or tracking history on our servers. Your browser communicates directly with public data APIs.

2.2 Third-Party Services
This tool aggregates data from third-party services which may collect their own usage logs. By using this tool, you acknowledge traffic to:

  • OpenSky Network / adsb.lol: ADS-B flight data.
  • Global Fishing Watch: Maritime tracking data.
  • CelesTrak: NORAD satellite elements.
  • TeleGeography / Submarine Cable Map: Submarine cable network data.
  • Radio Browser API: Radio station streaming data and metadata.
  • RepeaterBook: Radio repeater directory data.
  • MeshMap.net: Meshtastic mesh network node data.
  • MeshCore.dev: MeshCore network node data.
  • The GDELT Project: Global news tracking and event monitoring.
  • Polymarket: Prediction markets and future analytics.
  • USGS Earthquake API: Global earthquake and seismic event data.
  • OpenFreeMap / MapLibre: Map tiles and rendering.

2.3 Cookies & Local Storage
We may use local browser storage (localStorage) solely to save your user preferences (e.g., theme settings, unit toggles). No tracking cookies are used for advertising purposes.
